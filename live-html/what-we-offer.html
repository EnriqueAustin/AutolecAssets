NATURE OF BUSINESS


Autolec Assets specializes in repairing and maintaining vehicles, machines, facilities and equipment used in agriculture and agricultural construction.

With our expertise in both electrical and mechanical work we provide fault finding and repair services for a broad spectrum of machinery and equipment.

We have taken the insight we've gained from working on agricultural equipment and applied it to our own manufacturing ventures, identifying and improving on common issues we've found servicing these machines.

Bringing new innovations and improved designs, we proudly offer manufacturing services of agricultural equipment such as baggers and de-baggers.
